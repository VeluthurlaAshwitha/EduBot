About EduBot

EduBot is an AI-powered chatbot designed to assist students, faculty, and visitors with information about our campus. Using natural language processing, EduBot can understand and respond to a wide range of queries efficiently.

We offer two versions: a rule-based chatbot for common questions and an advanced AI chatbot powered by Gemini for more complex inquiries.
